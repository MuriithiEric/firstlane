Application of a PESEL number

This should be the first formality each student should complete as soon as they have been in Poland for 60 days or more PESEL (Polish: Powszechny Elektroniczny System Ewidencji Ludności, Universal Electronic System for Registration of the Population) is the national identification number in Poland. It consists of 11 digits that are unique for every registered person. It looks like this: YYMMDDPPPPK.
  • YY: year of birth
  • MM: month of birth
  • DD: day of birth
  • PPPP: random number, but the last digit indicates person's gender (odd number is for male, even for female)
  • K: randomly generated number

Registration of Home Address

In Poland there is an obligation, for both Poles and foreigners, to have their address here registered if their stay exceeds 30 days period (for non-EU citizens) or 3 months (for EU citizens). In Polish it is called ZAMELDOWANIE. To fulfill that obligation, you must visit district office (pol. urząd dzielnicy) competent for your place of residence with:
  • Rental agreement
  • Passport
  • Visaor Residence Card

Temporary Residence Card

This is a very important document that Every Non-EU student needs to make their stay legal in Poland. This can be quite a complicated process and we highly recommend you use a lawyer to ensure a positive decision. We will notify you when it is time to apply for your residence card. We recommend applying two months before expiry of your visa. You should apply for a Temporary Residence Permit before your visa expires. When you receive the permit, you will be issued a Temporary Residence Card, an ID card that proves that you have a residence permit. Together with your travel document, it allows you to cross the Polish border as many times as you wish. The first residence permit is issued for a period of 15 months. It can be renewed for periods of up to 3 years but for no longer than the remainder of your studies. REMEMBER: You should apply for the Temporary Residence Card when you are legally staying in Poland, which means after you arrive, but before the expiry of your visa. Don’t worry we will as part of our package we will inform you when your visa is nearing expiry so we can begin the application process.
