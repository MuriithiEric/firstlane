Studying in Poland is attractive not only academically, but also for the high quality of life available here. The country's economy has seen rapid growth over the past 20 years. It is a modern, clean, very safe and offers all the opportunities available elsewhere. This at a cost of living still much lower than in Western Europe, and international students are enrolling here in numbers. Accommodation

The average cost of a place in a public university dormitory is PLN 400-600, in a private dormitory PLN 800-1300, the price of a room to rent in a shared apartment PLN 900-1300, and the rent of a studio flat PLN 1300-2000. An apartment with 2-3 rooms costs 2000-3000 PLN.
